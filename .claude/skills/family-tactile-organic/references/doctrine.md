# Doctrine

## Thesis
Create warmth and approachability through rounded geometry, gentle surfaces, and soft visual transitions without losing clarity.

## Visual grammar
- rounded grouping
- warm or ambient color fields
- soft depth with clear hierarchy
- approachable pacing
- playful but controlled accents

## Typography
- typography should feel friendly and readable
- avoid severe contrast in tone unless needed for hierarchy
- keep labels and body text simple and welcoming

## Components
- buttons, cards, and inputs should feel inviting
- surfaces can feel material or cushioned
- onboarding and feature steps should avoid harsh jumps

## Motion
Use restrained-to-playful motion. Support ease and friendliness, not distraction.

## Anti-patterns
- softness with low contrast
- blobs that erase structure
- toy-like excess in serious products
- puffy UI with no hierarchy
- gradients doing all the branding work
