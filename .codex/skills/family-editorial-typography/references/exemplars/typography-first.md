# Typography First

## Thesis
Treat type as the main visual material so hierarchy, voice, and rhythm are carried by the headline system before imagery or surfaces take over.

## Best when
- the brief says typography-led, expressive type, oversized headline, or voice-driven brand
- editorial platforms, launches, studios, or culture-led brands need a stronger point of view
- the page can let text become the hero
- motion tolerance is low to medium

## Distinguishing signals
- more headline-dominant than `editorial-minimalism`
- less calm and understated than `meaningful-minimalism`
- less neutral and luxurious than `premium-monochrome`
- uses typographic scale and voice as the primary spectacle

## Layout directives
- let one or two major text moments dominate the page
- build sections around typographic hierarchy instead of card systems
- use whitespace to support oversized type, not to dilute it
- keep supporting content clearly subordinate to the headline system

## Typography and surface directives
- use expressive display and readable body pairings
- allow stronger scale jumps than other editorial directions
- keep surfaces restrained so text remains the main form
- use accents only when they reinforce the typographic voice

## Component behavior
- CTA can sit closer to the headline logic than to standard button-heavy marketing
- cards should stay minimal and secondary
- proof should support the voice of the page, not flatten it
- imagery should act as an accent or counterweight, not the primary system

## Avoid
- treating typography as decoration with weak hierarchy underneath
- dense operational UI that fights oversized type
- too much chrome around already expressive headlines
- sacrificing readability for typographic drama

## Review and conversion cues
- If the page feels mostly calm and spacious rather than type-led, it is drifting toward `editorial-minimalism`.
- If it feels expensive but visually quiet, it is drifting toward `premium-monochrome`.
- To convert from `editorial-minimalism`, enlarge the role of display type before adding other drama.
