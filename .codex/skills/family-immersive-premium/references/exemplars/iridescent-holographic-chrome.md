# Iridescent Holographic Chrome

## Thesis
Use reflective materiality, chrome sheen, and iridescent highlights to create fashion-tech or campaign energy where the material surface itself is the spectacle.

## Best when
- the brief says chrome, holographic, iridescent, reflective, metallic, beauty-tech, music-tech, or fashion-tech
- the page needs novelty and gloss more than dark atmosphere
- the hero should feel material and art-directed
- medium motion is acceptable

## Distinguishing signals
- more reflective and fashion-coded than `dark-glow-aurora`
- less system-UI oriented than `glassmorphism-mature`
- more material-surface focused than `motion-led-storytelling`
- can overlap with Y2K, but should stay more premium than nostalgic

## Layout directives
- keep the structure relatively simple so the material language can lead
- build one hero focal surface, not many competing shiny elements
- use strong framing around the primary statement or product visual
- keep supporting sections cleaner than the hero

## Typography and surface directives
- typography should support the material drama, not outcompete it
- use reflective or iridescent accents sparingly but intentionally
- prefer soft light backgrounds or light-neutral canvases over mandatory dark mode
- keep supporting surfaces controlled so chrome remains special

## Component behavior
- hero visuals can carry the novelty load
- CTAs should remain readable and structurally plain enough to survive shine
- cards and panels should not all become reflective
- badges or pills can hold subtle iridescent accents if they preserve contrast

## Avoid
- turning every surface into chrome
- drifting into generic dark-glow futurism
- letting novelty reduce trust or readability
- leaning so hard into retro gloss that it becomes `y2k-retro-futurism`

## Review and conversion cues
- If the page feels dark, neon, and cinematic, it is drifting toward `dark-glow-aurora`.
- If it feels playful-nostalgic rather than premium-reflective, it is drifting toward `y2k-retro-futurism`.
- To convert from `dark-glow-aurora`, brighten the canvas and shift spectacle from glow to reflective materiality.
