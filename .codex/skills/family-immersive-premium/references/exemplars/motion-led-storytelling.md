# Motion Led Storytelling

## Thesis
Organize the page like a reveal sequence so hierarchy, pacing, and section transitions imply motion and narrative progression even when effects stay restrained.

## Best when
- the brief says scrollytelling, reveal, sticky narrative, launch story, or cinematic pacing
- brand launches, campaign microsites, or interactive reports need narrative flow
- the page should feel like a guided sequence rather than a static layout
- motion tolerance is medium to high

## Distinguishing signals
- more pacing-led than `immersive-3d-product`
- less material-specific than `glassmorphism-mature`
- less dark-atmosphere dependent than `dark-glow-aurora`
- more narrative-structured than `iridescent-holographic-chrome`

## Layout directives
- build sections as chapters in a reveal journey
- use sticky or sequence-minded composition logic even if the implementation stays static
- let each section hand off to the next with clear escalation or payoff
- keep one dominant narrative spine so the page does not feel like disconnected scenes

## Typography and surface directives
- use typography to mark narrative beats, not just sections
- keep surfaces supportive to the reveal structure rather than over-stylized
- let accents emphasize transitions and moments of payoff
- maintain enough contrast to keep the story readable at every stage

## Component behavior
- hero sets the first narrative promise rather than showing everything immediately
- CTA can arrive after tension has been built
- feature blocks should feel sequenced, not interchangeable
- proof can act as narrative validation rather than generic social proof

## Avoid
- wow-factor overpowering the story
- motion ideas with no hierarchy purpose
- one long page of equally intense scenes
- using narrative pacing for task-heavy or utilitarian pages

## Review and conversion cues
- If the page is really about one hero object, it is drifting toward `immersive-3d-product`.
- If spectacle comes mainly from glow or dark atmosphere, it is drifting toward `dark-glow-aurora`.
- To convert from static premium pages, clarify the chapter flow before adding more motion language.
