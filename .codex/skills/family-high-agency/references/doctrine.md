# Doctrine

## Thesis
Use asymmetry, decisive hierarchy, and momentum shifts to make a page feel like a flagship launch instead of a safe template.

## Visual grammar
- one dominant focal move per section
- asymmetry with purpose
- contrast through placement before decoration
- strong CTA energy
- proof blocks with weight

## Typography
- direct, confident headlines
- fewer but stronger size jumps
- body copy stays functional, not literary

## Components
- hero should not sit politely in the center
- CTA should feel intentional and timely
- feature sections should vary composition
- proof should look earned, not pasted in

## Motion
Use restrained-to-supportive motion. Favor reveals that reinforce hierarchy, not spectacle.

## Anti-patterns
- centered generic hero
- equal-weight sections throughout
- repeated 3-column feature grids
- decorative cards with no narrative role
- bold palette without stronger structure
