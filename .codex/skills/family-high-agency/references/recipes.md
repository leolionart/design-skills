# Recipes

## Exemplar chooser
- `default-high-agency` -> use for flagship launches, asymmetry, founder confidence, and anti-generic product marketing that should stay bold but controlled.

## Quick differentiation rules
- Choose `default-high-agency` over `neo-brutalism` when the page should stay launch-focused and strategic rather than hard-edged or graphic-first.
- Choose `default-high-agency` over `dark-glow-aurora` when hierarchy and momentum matter more than cinematic atmosphere.
- Choose `default-high-agency` over editorial directions when CTA timing, proof weight, and decisive section contrast matter more than reading calm.

## Safe blends
- `default-high-agency` + editorial-typography for sharper premium restraint
- `default-high-agency` + immersive-premium for one stronger hero spectacle moment
- `default-high-agency` + grid-product for clearer product modules below the fold

## Escalation rule
Use this family when the brief wants launch energy with control. Open the exemplar pack only after confirming the page should feel decisive, asymmetric, and non-generic rather than experimental, editorial, or cinematic.
