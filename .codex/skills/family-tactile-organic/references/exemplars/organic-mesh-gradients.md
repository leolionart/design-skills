# Organic Mesh Gradients

## Thesis
Use ambient color fields, blobs, and soft diffusion to create a current, gentle, digitally native mood where atmosphere softens the interface without removing structure.

## Best when
- the brief says mesh gradient, blob, ambient, soft futurism, or warm digital brand
- startups, wellness tech, AI, and creator tools need softness without heavy tactility
- the page should feel current and approachable through atmosphere
- motion tolerance is low

## Distinguishing signals
- more atmospheric than `claymorphism-soft-3d`
- softer and more ambient than `clean-saas-gradient`
- less punchy and character-led than `cute-alism-kawaii-brutalism`
- uses color field and light diffusion more than material form

## Layout directives
- keep the structure simple so ambient backgrounds can support rather than confuse it
- use sections with calm flow and clear grouping
- let one or two ambient fields carry the mood instead of decorating every block
- preserve obvious hierarchy over the gradients

## Typography and surface directives
- keep typography clear and friendly, not severe
- use gradients as atmosphere, not as the primary information layer
- favor light, translucent, or gently framed surfaces
- keep contrast discipline explicit where text crosses color fields

## Component behavior
- buttons and cards should stay readable above the ambient background
- hero can use one stronger gradient field as the emotional anchor
- proof and feature sections should restore structure after the soft hero mood
- illustrations or abstract shapes can support brand softness without overcrowding the page

## Avoid
- blobs obscuring layout structure
- insufficient contrast on text and controls
- turning the whole page into decorative atmosphere
- material softness that should really become `claymorphism-soft-3d`

## Review and conversion cues
- If the page feels molded, puffy, and touchable, it is drifting toward `claymorphism-soft-3d`.
- If the direction becomes more polished SaaS than soft atmosphere, it is drifting toward `clean-saas-gradient`.
- To convert from colder product pages, introduce one ambient field first before adding more organic treatment.
